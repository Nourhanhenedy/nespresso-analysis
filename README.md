 # **☕ Nespresso Sales Analysis Dashboard (2025-2026)**

This repository contains a comprehensive Power BI Dashboard analyzing

sales performance, customer behavior, and regional trends for Nespresso. 

The report provides actionable insights into revenue streams across

different channels, categories, and geographical regions.


******************************************************************************

## 📊 Project Overview

The dashboard is split into two primary views:

#### Home (2025 Insights):

A deep dive into historical revenue, monthly trends, and product performance.

#### Customer Analysis:

Focused on order frequency, retention rates, and channel-specific Average Order Value (AOV).


******************************************************************************


## 🔑 Key Insights & KPIs

### Sales Performance

#### Total Revenue (2025):

44K with a total of 417 orders.

#### Top Region:

Sicilia leads the revenue charts ($10K in customer view, $9K in 2025 view), followed consistently by Toscana and Piemonte.

#### Seasonality:

Sales show a peak in the early months (January/September) with a gradual decline toward the end of the year.

### Product & Channel Analysis

#### Top Category:

Accessories and Machines are the primary drivers of order volume.

#### Channel Efficiency: 

While Desktop often leads in total order volume, the Boutique channel maintains the highest Average Order Value (AOV) at approximately 116.

#### Revenue Mix: 

Mobile App revenue remains competitive at roughly 15.63K (2025 data).

## Customer Behavior

#### Customer Retention: 

An impressive 60.48% returning rate.

#### Average Order Value:

$105.59 (2025) vs $1.33 (Units/Frequency per customer).

#### Daily Trends: 

Order volume is highest on Saturdays and Mondays, dropping significantly toward Thursdays.
